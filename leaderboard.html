Getting Started

Quicket is a way to share with those around you. By sharing your location, we provide a minimalistic way to anonymously post within your radius.
If you see something you like, you can click to hop a post up to the top.
Our goal is to provide a consistent and reliable experience for our users, using the latest technology and methodology for precise location positioning
